Favorites

  • Berserk
    by Kentaro Miura — carving your way through demons and trauma in search of freedom and meaning.
  • Blame!
    by Tsutomu Nihei — in a system that doesn't care about you, meaning comes from persistence.
  • NieR (not a book but still meaningful)
    by Yoko Taro — When the purpose you're given collapses, you get to choose your own meaning.
